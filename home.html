Welcome to the Crime Alert Application

This application will provide you and other users relevant safety information according to your location! With more and more safety issues being addressed by campus police for thefts, threats, and other misdemeanors, we seek to provide users peace of mind for where they live and where they travel to.

Searching Crimesclose

Search for crimes in your area using our interactive map. Crimes are crowdsourced by users like yourself and verified by the police. Enter a crime or location in the search bar on the map page to see dangerous areas.

Report A Crimeclose

If you've recently witnessed a crime in an area, you can report directly to your campus police on the report page. Simply enter the date, time, type of crime, description and location. This service is completely anonymous to facilitate your comfort. As a reminder, this is not a substitute for emergency services, call 911 if you need urgent help.

Note that this is not a replacement for 911! Please use this application once you are out of an emergency situation. A police officer will review reports you submit and distribute them to the rest of this systems' users.
