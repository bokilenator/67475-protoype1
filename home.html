Welcome to the Crime Alert Application

This application will provide you and other users relevant safety information according to your location! With more and more safety issues being addressed by campus police for thefts, threats, and other misdemeanors, we seek to provide users peace of mind where they live and travel to.

The Crime Alert Application enables users to self-report crimes. Crowdsourcing crime reports provides immediate feedback to the community with much greater accuracy of the crime location through a pin based system that one can drop on the map using their exact current location.

Note that this is not a replacement for 911! Please use this application once you are out of an emergency situation. A police officer will review reports you submit and distribute them to the rest of this systems' users.

Ready to report a crime or view crimes in your area? Click the menu icon to get started!
